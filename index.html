Ayushman Bharat Health Account
What is ABHA?

ABHA is a 14-digit unique health identity issued by the Government of India. It lets every citizen securely store, access, and share their medical records — prescriptions, lab reports, and doctor visits — digitally, from anywhere.

With ABHA, doctors can instantly access your health history, reducing repeated tests and improving care — especially important in rural settings where paper records are easily lost.

World Health Organization
About WHO

The World Health Organization (WHO) is the United Nations agency responsible for international public health. It sets global health standards, coordinates responses to health emergencies, and provides evidence-based guidance. WHO is a key partner in achieving SDG 3 — ensuring healthy lives and well-being for all people of all ages.
